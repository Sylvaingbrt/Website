Sobera



An app that use your camera and the supported filters.
IMPORTANT: you need a SD card.


Swipe right and left to change filters.
Tap once to see buttons and info.
If you take a picture, wait a moment before a change of interface.
When you see a question appear and two buttons on the bottom of your phone, choose 'Sobel' to save a copy of your picture with edge detection filter.

DOWNLOAD

Known bug: app crash after first permission for external storage.
App logo is not set.
On front camera, when a picture is taken, the view is weird.

To intsall an app on your own android device from here, you can follow those steps:


- Open your browser with your android, find the APK file you want to download, and tap it (you should then be able to see it downloading on the top bar of your device).
- Once it's downloaded, open Downloads, tap on the APK file, and tap Yes when prompted. (Change settings if needed)
- The app will begin installing on your device.
